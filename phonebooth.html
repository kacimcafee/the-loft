How the Story Booth Came to Be

The Loft proudly hosts southern Colorado's first ever Story Booth, an upcycled phonebooth that shares and preserves the memories of locals at the heart of Manitou Springs. The booth now contains a directory with two dozen stories, creating a sense of commoradory across the air waves.
