How the Story Booth Came to Be

Justin Snyder, co-owner of The Loft, grew up with a deep love for stories and the forgotten corners of the past. As a child, he would call the local library each week just to hear a new tale through an old phone line. That memory sparked an idea: a storytelling phone booth, a place where imagination still exists.
